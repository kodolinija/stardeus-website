<p>Most resources can be traded with ships, space stations, and spaceports. Occasionally, a merchant can land on your ship in a <span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/database/objects/devices/stasis-pod/">Stasis Pod</a></span>.</p>

<figure class="codex-video"><video src="/vid/codex/Production/Trade.webm" controls loop muted playsinline preload="metadata"></video></figure>
<p>Faction relationships can affect price markup.</p>

<p>Prices are mainly driven by the <span class="accent"><span class="accent"><b>Stock Market</b></span></span>, which reacts to supply and demand. Local availability and faction relationships can also affect the final price of items you buy and sell.</p>

<p>If you're stranded, you can use the <span class="nobr"><span class="accent">Online Shop</span></span> to get basic commodities delivered directly to your ship via a <span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/database/obj/containers/storage-capsule/">Storage Capsule</a></span>. This service is more expensive than regular trading.</p>
