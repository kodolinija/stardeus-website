<p>There are several types of <span class="accent"><span class="accent"><b>Ship Weapons</b></span></span>, each with its own strengths and weaknesses.</p>

<p>Ship weapons must be assigned to <span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/manual/ship-combat/battle-stations/">Battle Stations</a></span> before they can operate.</p>

<p><span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/database/objects/devices/shredder-cannon/">Ballistic Weapons</a></span> are small caliber guns that are stopped by the <span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/manual/ship-combat/shields/">Shields</a></span>. They generate <span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/manual/ship-combat/flux/">Hard Flux</a></span> upon impact with the <span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/manual/ship-combat/shields/">Shields</a></span>, and can cause significant damage for unshielded ships.</p>

<p><span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/database/objects/devices/shredder-cannon/">Ballistic Weapons</a></span> are also effective at shooting down slow-moving projectiles, such as <span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/database/obj/asteroids/asteroid01/">Asteroid</a></span>.</p>

<p><span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/database/objects/devices/heavy-laser/">Laser Weapons</a></span> are more powerful than <span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/database/objects/devices/shredder-cannon/">Ballistic Weapons</a></span> but recharge slowly.</p>

<p><span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/database/objects/devices/rocket-pod/">Missile Weapons</a></span> are extremely dangerous because they ignore <span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/manual/ship-combat/shields/">Shields</a></span> and deal significant damage. Their main drawback is the need for expensive ammo.</p>

<p><span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/database/objects/devices/interceptor-turret/">Point Defense Weapons</a></span> cannot attack other ships, but can be used to shoot down incoming projectiles of <span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/database/objects/devices/rocket-pod/">Missile Weapons</a></span>. They also work great against non-ship threats such as <span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/database/obj/containers/breach-capsule-flying/">Breach Capsule</a></span>.</p>

<p><span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/database/objects/devices/gauss-striker/">Plasma Weapons</a></span> are ultimate weapons that can overwhelm the <span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/manual/ship-combat/shields/">Shields</a></span> and deal massive damage.</p>
