<p>Evade determines how likely the ship will dodge incoming attacks. Smaller ships have higher evade value than larger ships.</p>

<p>Each <span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/database/objects/devices/thruster/">Thruster</a></span> adds some <span class="accent"><span class="accent"><b>Evade</b></span></span>, making your ship harder to hit. <span class="accent"><span class="accent"><b>Evade</b></span></span> depends on your <span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/manual/ship-combat/ship-class/">Ship Class</a></span> and size, and is limited to <span class="accent"><span class="accent"><b>50%</b></span></span>.</p>
