<p>Flux is a buildup of heat energy during combat. It rises when firing weapons or blocking attacks with shields. Too much Flux disables key systems.</p>

<p>There are two types of <span class="accent"><span class="accent"><b>Flux</b></span></span>:<br> <span style="font-size:75%">→</span> <span class="accent"><span class="accent"><b>Soft Flux</b></span></span> is generated when you fire your weapons. It vents automatically over time.<br> <span style="font-size:75%">→</span> <span class="accent"><span class="accent"><b>Hard Flux</b></span></span> is generated by absorbing enemy hits with your <span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/manual/ship-combat/shields/">Shields</a></span>. It does not vent automatically.<br></p>

<p>Some weapons generate <span class="accent"><span class="accent"><b>Hard Flux</b></span></span> for your ship when fired. Check individual weapon stats for more details.</p>

<p><span class="accent"><span class="accent"><b>Accumulated Flux</b></span></span> consists of <span class="accent"><span class="accent"><b>Hard Flux</b></span></span> + <span class="accent"><span class="accent"><b>Soft Flux</b></span></span>. The amount you can accumulate is limited by your <span class="accent"><span class="accent"><b>Flux Capacity</b></span></span>.</p>
