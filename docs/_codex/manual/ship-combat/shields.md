<p><span class="accent"><span class="accent"><b>Shields</b></span></span> are the most important defense mechanisms at your disposal. At least one <span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/database/objects/devices/shield-emitter/">Shield Emitter</a></span> is required for a functioning shield.</p>

<p><span class="accent"><span class="accent"><b>Shields</b></span></span> can protect your ship from incoming projectiles. Absorbing hits with <span class="accent"><span class="accent"><b>Shields</b></span></span> generates <span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/manual/ship-combat/flux/">Hard Flux</a></span>.</p>

<p><b><span style="color:#F95858">Warning</span></b>: Building more than one <span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/database/objects/devices/shield-emitter/">Shield Emitter</a></span> does not make shields stronger or faster. Extra emitters only provide redundancy at a high <span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/manual/ship-combat/class-points/">Class Points</a></span> cost. If the last <span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/database/objects/devices/shield-emitter/">Shield Emitter</a></span> is disabled, shields are lost.</p>
