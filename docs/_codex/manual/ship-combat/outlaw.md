<p>Exceeding the allowed <span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/manual/ship-combat/class-points/">Class Points</a></span> for your <span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/manual/ship-combat/ship-class/">Ship Class</a></span> is illegal and results in <span class="accent"><span class="accent"><b>Outlaw</b></span></span> status.</p>

<p>A notification will inform you when you violate the <span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/manual/ship-combat/class-points/">Class Points</a></span> limit.</p>

<p>Being an <span class="accent"><span class="accent"><b>Outlaw</b></span></span> has drawbacks. Most traders will avoid you, and <span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/manual/ship-combat/law-enforcement/">Law Enforcement</a></span> officers will hunt you.</p>
