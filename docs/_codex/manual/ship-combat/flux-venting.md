<p>If <span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/manual/ship-combat/flux/">Accumulated Flux</a></span> exceeds your <span class="accent"><span class="accent"><b>Flux Capacity</b></span></span>, a <span class="accent"><span class="accent"><b>Flux Overload</b></span></span> will happen.</p>

<p><span class="accent"><span class="accent"><b>Flux Overload</b></span></span> will disable your <span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/manual/ship-combat/shields/">Shields</a></span> and force venting until all <span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/manual/ship-combat/flux/">Flux</a></span> is purged.</p>

<p>Vent <span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/manual/ship-combat/flux/">Flux</a></span> manually to prevent <span class="accent"><span class="accent"><b>Flux Overload</b></span></span>. Manual venting disables shields, but removes flux faster than forced venting during <span class="accent"><span class="accent"><b>Flux Overload</b></span></span>. You can stop manual venting at any time.</p>

<p>Build <span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/database/objects/devices/flux-vent/">Flux Vents</a></span> to increase the flux venting rate.</p>
