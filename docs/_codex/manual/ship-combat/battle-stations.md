<p><span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/database/objects/devices/battle-station/">Battle Stations</a></span> control ship weapons. One battle station can control multiple weapons of the same type. Adding more weapons may require additional battle stations.</p>

<p>Assign a weapon type to each <span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/database/objects/devices/battle-station/">Battle Station</a></span>. You can also configure which targets the weapons controlled by that station will engage.</p>
