<p>Selecting a being will reveal any active or developing <span class="accent"><b>Conditions</b></span>, including <span class="accent"><b>Wounds</b></span> from physical injuries. It can help identify the <span class="accent"><b>cause of death</b></span> as well.</p>

<p>Some conditions, such as <span class="accent"><b>Depression</b></span>, have mental effects, while others, like <span class="accent"><b>Hypoxia</b></span>, can cause physical injuries that can lead to <img class="inline-icon" src="/img/codex/sprites/glyphs/glyphs-icons-color/skull.png" alt="Skull"> <span class="accent"><b>Death</b></span>.</p>

<p>Most conditions have an underlying <span class="accent"><b>root cause</b></span>. Fixing the cause allows the condition to <span class="accent"><b>recover</b></span> over time.</p>
<p>Example: <span class="accent"><b>Depression</b></span> develops when the <span class="accent"><b>Fun</b></span> stat remains low. Providing <span class="accent"><b>Entertainment</b></span> facilities can increase <span class="accent"><b>Fun</b></span> and gradually reduce <span class="accent"><b>Depression</b></span>.</p>
