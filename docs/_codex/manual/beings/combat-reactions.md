<p>Each <span class="accent"><b>friendly being</b></span> has one of the following <span class="accent"><b>Combat Reactions</b></span>:<br> - <span class="accent"><b>Free Will</b></span>: The being will act in their <span class="accent"><b>best interest</b></span> based on the <span class="accent"><b>situation</b></span>.<br> - <span class="accent"><b>Fight</b></span>: The being will actively <span class="accent"><b>seek</b></span> out enemies and <span class="accent"><b>engage</b></span> them.<br> - <span class="accent"><b>Flee</b></span>: The being will attempt to <span class="accent"><b>run away</b></span> from enemies.<br> - <span class="accent"><b>Ignore</b></span>: The being will focus on <span class="accent"><b>colony tasks</b></span> and personal <span class="accent"><b>needs</b></span>, ignoring enemies unless <span class="accent"><b>provoked</b></span> or given a <span class="accent"><b>Direct Order</b></span>.</p>
