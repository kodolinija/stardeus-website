<p><span class="accent"><span class="accent"><b>Cryostability</b></span></span> <span style="font-size:75%">→</span> Shows how stable the life support systems are. Unstable cryochambers will eject colonists more frequently and with worse initial conditions. To increase stability, switch the life support from basic to advanced. It will consume a lot of energy, but stability will slowly improve.</p>

<p><span class="accent"><span class="accent"><b>Advanced Life Support</b></span></span> <span style="font-size:75%">→</span> Basic life support level will consume a small amount of electricity to keep the cryostability from deteriorating completely. Advanced life support level will gradually increase the stability and allow extra control over the being wake-up rate.</p>

<p><span class="accent"><span class="accent"><b>Prevent Malfunctions</b></span></span> <span style="font-size:75%">→</span> Consume additional electricity to prevent pod malfunctions that cause colonist wake-ups. Full effect is achieved when cryo-stability is at least <span class="accent"><b>90%</b></span>.</p>
