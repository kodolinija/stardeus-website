<p><span class="nobr"><kbd title="Keyboard: F">F</kbd></span> <span class="accent"><span class="accent"><b>Draft</b></span></span> workers and colonists to control them directly during combat. While drafted, they will not perform colony tasks.</p>

<p>Use <span class="nobr"><kbd title="Mouse: Right Button">Right Button</kbd></span> to order drafted beings to move to a specific location or attack a specific enemy.</p>

<p>Remember to <span class="nobr"><kbd title="Keyboard: F">F</kbd></span> <span class="accent"><span class="accent"><b>Undraft</b></span></span> them after the combat is over.</p>
