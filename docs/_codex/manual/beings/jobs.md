<p>Each colony task has a job category. A worker must be capable of that job type to perform the task. Some past experiences and traits may make certain beings incapable of performing specific jobs.</p>

<figure class="codex-image"><img src="/img/codex/UI/CantAssign.png" alt="Tasks, Jobs, and Skills - CantAssign" loading="lazy"></figure>
<p>You can configure which job types each being is allowed to perform.</p>

<figure class="codex-image"><img src="/img/codex/UI/Jobs.png" alt="Tasks, Jobs, and Skills - Jobs" loading="lazy"></figure>
<p>Job capability is not the only requirement for performing a task. Most tasks also require one or more abilities, which a being may or may not have. For example, cleaning solar panels requires the ability to fly, so only someone with a <span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/database/obj/parts/mobilizer/mini-jet/">Mini Jet</a></span> or <span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/database/obj/equipment/clothing/space-suit02/">Jet Suit</a></span> can do the job, even if they are highly skilled at cleaning.</p>

<figure class="codex-image"><img src="/img/codex/UI/Abilities.png" alt="Tasks, Jobs, and Skills - Abilities" loading="lazy"></figure>
<p>A being's intelligence level, provided by its core or brain, can also limit the type of work it can perform. For example, a being with a <span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/database/obj/parts/core/dumb-core/">Dumb Core</a></span> or <span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/database/obj/parts/core/lite-core/">Lite Core</a></span> cannot operate a <span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/database/objects/devices/research-station/">Research Station</a></span>, but someone with a <span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/database/obj/parts/core/mini-core/">Mini Core</a></span> or <span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/database/obj/parts/brain/brain-human/">Human Brain</a></span> can.</p>

<p>Most jobs have a skill level. Level 0 means the being is an amateur and may make mistakes. Higher skill can improve proficiency, success chance, and speed. Robot worker skills can be trained at an <span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/database/objects/devices/ml-booth/">ML Booth</a></span>, but this requires removing the core, which carries some risk.</p>

<p>You can manage everyone's jobs in <span class="nobr"><span class="accent">Ship Computer → Apps → Jobs</span></span>.</p>

<figure class="codex-image"><img src="/img/codex/UI/JobMatrix.png" alt="Tasks, Jobs, and Skills - JobMatrix" loading="lazy"></figure>
<p>To force-assign a task, select a worker, then press <kbd>Mouse2</kbd> over the task location and use the popup menu to assign it. The being must be able to reach the location, perform the task, and must not be drafted.</p>
