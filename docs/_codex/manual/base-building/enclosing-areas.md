<p>Build floors inside the perimeter you want to enclose.</p>

<p>Build walls around the area you want to enclose.</p>

<p>Consult the <span class="nobr"><span class="accent">Rooms</span></span> overlay to see which areas are considered enclosed.</p>

<p>Use the <span class="nobr"><span class="accent">Airtightness</span></span> and <span class="nobr"><span class="accent">Insulation</span></span> overlays to find floor and wall gaps.</p>
