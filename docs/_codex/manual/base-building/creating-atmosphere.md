<p>Ensure the area is enclosed and pressurized first. See: <span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/manual/base-building/enclosing-areas/">Enclosing Areas</a></span>, <span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/manual/base-building/pressurizing-areas/">Pressurizing Areas</a></span></p>

<p>Build and connect one or more <span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/database/objects/devices/heater-mini/">Heater</a></span> and <span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/database/objects/devices/oxygen-pump-mini/">Oxygen Pump</a></span> to cover the area.</p>

<p>It can take some time to fully oxygenate and heat the area. Consult the <span class="nobr"><span class="accent">Oxygen</span></span> and <span class="nobr"><span class="accent">Temperature</span></span> overlays to determine whether you need additional heaters or oxygen pumps.</p>

<p>Consult the <span class="nobr"><span class="accent">Airtightness</span></span> and <span class="nobr"><span class="accent">Insulation</span></span> overlays to find gaps and leaks.</p>
