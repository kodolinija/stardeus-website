<p>To prevent electric grid disruptions, plan your grid with extra redundancy. This is especially important for critical pieces of your infrastructure, like Reactors, Batteries, the Ship Computer, the Stasis Array, Oxygen Pumps, Heaters, Doors, and Airlocks.</p>
<p>&gt;To increase redundancy, build more connectors and have important devices connected through more than one connection.</p>
<p>&gt;High Availability implies that even if some part of your grid is destroyed, there is zero or minimal connectivity loss for critical pieces of your infrastructure.</p>
