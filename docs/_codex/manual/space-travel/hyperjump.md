<p><span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/database/objects/devices/fusion-drive/">Fusion Drive</a></span> enables entry into hyperspace and allows short-range hyperjumps to adjacent sectors, consuming <span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/database/obj/materials/fusion-cell/">Fusion Cell</a></span> as fuel.</p>

<p><span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/database/objects/devices/solar-panel/">Solar Panels</a></span> do not generate power while in hyperspace. Ensure you have backup reactors and batteries to stay powered at all times.</p>
