<p>Datoids represent the information that is used for unlocking new technologies in the <span class="accent"><b>Research</b></span>.</p>
<p>&gt;Datoids are produced by processing mostly common resources in <span class="accent"><b>Data Harvesters</b></span>. There are several types of Data Harvesters that can produce different kinds of Datoids.</p>
<p>&gt;Datoids must be stored in <span class="accent"><b>Datoid Loaders</b></span> to become usable for research.</p>
<p>&gt;Datoids cannot be bought or sold.</p>
