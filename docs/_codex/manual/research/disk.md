<p>Unlocking new research consumes disk space. Your available capacity is shown in the top-left corner of the <span class="nobr"><span class="accent">Research Tree</span></span>.</p>

<figure class="codex-image"><img src="/img/codex/Research/Disk.png" alt="Research Disk Storage - Disk" loading="lazy"></figure>
<p>Build and connect additional <span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/database/objects/devices/disk-module-mini/">Disk Modules</a></span> to increase your disk space.</p>

<figure class="codex-image"><img src="/img/codex/Research/Disk2.png" alt="Research Disk Storage - Disk2" loading="lazy"></figure>
<p>Install <span class="nobr"><a href="/codex/database/obj/upgrades/disk-compression-upgrade/">Disk Compression Upgrade</a></span> to increase the capacity of existing disk modules.</p>
